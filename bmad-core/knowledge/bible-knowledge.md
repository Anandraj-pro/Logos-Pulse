## Bible Reading Plans (Referenced in Weekly Assignment)

### 30-Day New Testament Starter
Days 1-30 covering: Matthew 1-7 → John → Acts → Romans → Ephesians → Philippians → James → 1 John

### 90-Day Gospels & Epistles
Full New Testament in 90 days (~3 chapters/day)

### 365-Day Full Bible
Classic chronological plan: ~3.3 chapters/day to complete in one year

### Psalms & Proverbs Monthly
Psalm of the day (150 over 5 months) + Proverb of the day (31/month)

### Prayer Warrior Focus Plan
Emphasis on Daniel, Ephesians 6, Luke 11, Acts, Revelation — for intercessors

## Pastoral Wisdom Themes

### For Members Struggling with Consistency
> "Do not be weary in well-doing, for in due season you will reap if you do not give up." — Galatians 6:9

### For Members Facing Doubt
> "If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask God, who gives generously to all without reproach." — James 1:5

### For Members Dealing with Sin
> "There is therefore now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus." — Romans 8:1

### For Members in Grief
> "The Lord is near to the brokenhearted and saves the crushed in spirit." — Psalm 34:18

### For Members Seeking Purpose
> "For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works." — Ephesians 2:10

### For Members Growing Cold
> "Remember therefore from where you have fallen; repent, and do the works you did at first." — Revelation 2:5
