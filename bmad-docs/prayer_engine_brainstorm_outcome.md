## DECISIONS MADE (Consensus)

### D1: Delivery Model — Hybrid with Guardrails
- **Self-service** is the default (members browse freely)
- **Pastor-prescribed** is first-class (not an afterthought)
- **Auto-assigned starter track** for new believers (system-driven, not manual)
- **Soft warnings** on Spiritual Warfare content (not hard restrictions)
- **Privacy boundary:** Pastors see engagement metrics for prescribed confessions, NOT self-selected browsing content

### D2: Schedule Model — 21-Day Plans as Primary
- **21-Day Plans** are the core structure (aligns with existing church rhythm)
- **AM/PM slots** within plans (morning confessions, evening declarations)
- **Ongoing mode** for daily identity/gratitude confessions
- **Flexible timing** — no guilt for inconsistency; celebrate ANY engagement
- **WhatsApp:** Single declaration line + scripture ref (not full text)

### D3: MVP Scope — Ship with Tier 1, Iterate
- Launch with **15-20 Tier 1 confessions** (don't wait for all 86)
- Add content over time as digitization and review completes
- Tier 1 must be live within **3 weeks** of development start

### D4: Content Standards — Non-Negotiable
- Every confession MUST have **2-3 scripture references**
- Language must be **first-person, present-tense declaration**
- **Theological review** by Bishop + 1 pastor before publication
- **Plain-language review** pass for accessibility (Pastor Emmanuel)
- **Short-form versions** alongside full text for mobile (Pastor Grace)

### D5: Content Management
- Pastors CAN create custom confessions for their members (flagged as "Pastor-created")
- Custom confessions do NOT enter the shared library without Bishop review
- Members can add **personal notes** alongside confessions but NOT create freestanding confessions
- **Confession of the Week** — Bishop-selected, pushed to all members, tied to Sunday sermon theme

### D6: Integration Priorities
| Priority | Integration |
|----------|------------|
| **Must (MVP)** | Daily Entry, Pastor Dashboard, WhatsApp Report |
| **Should (Phase 2)** | Prayer Journal deep link, Wizard Assignments |
| **Could (Phase 3)** | Streaks & Stats, Sermon Notes |

## KEY FEATURE ADDITIONS FROM SESSION

### F1: "What Are You Believing God For?" Entry Point
- First screen is NOT a category grid
- Simple question with quick-select chips: Healing, Finances, Family, Faith, Protection, Other
- Routes to relevant category with top confessions
- Full library browse available via "Explore All Categories"

### F2: "Confess Aloud" Mode (Bishop Priority)
- Focused reading view highlights one line at a time
- Large font, minimal UI, designed for speaking out loud
- Romans 10:10 principle — confession is spoken, not just read
- "Mark as Confessed" only after scrolling through all statements

### F3: New Believer Auto-Track
- 7-day foundational confession plan auto-assigned on registration
- No pastoral action needed — system-driven
- After 7 days, unlock full library with progressive disclosure

### F4: Community Encouragement Counter
- "47 believers are confessing with you this week" (anonymous count)
- Builds faith and reduces isolation
- Phase 2 feature, not MVP

### F5: Personal Reflection Journaling
- After confessing, optional prompt: "What spoke to you today?"
- Saved alongside confession completion, linked to Prayer Journal
- Private to the member (not visible to pastor)

### F6: Confession of the Week
- Bishop selects one confession weekly, tied to Sunday sermon
- Appears at top of every member's Prayer Engine
- Saturday push: preview for Sunday. Monday: starts daily plan for the week
- Bridges digital and physical church experience
