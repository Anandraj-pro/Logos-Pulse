## Pre-Session Context (Share 2 Days Before)

We have **86 confession/prayer documents** across 14 life categories collected over the years. Currently, Logos Pulse has 5 basic prayer templates. The Prayer Engine will transform this rich library into a living, accessible system that serves every member — from new believers to mature prayer warriors.

**Current State:**
- 5 standard templates (Morning, Evening, Warfare, Financial, Healing)
- Prayer Journal with confessions + declarations support
- Pastor can view shared prayers from members

**Proposed State:**
- 50+ curated confession/prayer templates across 14+ life categories
- Smart matching based on member needs
- Pastor-prescribed confession plans
- Daily confession schedules with AM/PM rotation

### Part 2: Delivery Model (30 min)

#### 2.1 How Should Members Access Confessions? (15 min)

**Option A: Self-Service Browse**
- Members browse categories, pick confessions they need
- Like a spiritual library — full freedom
- Risk: New believers may not know what they need

**Option B: Pastor-Prescribed**
- Pastors assign specific confessions to members based on counseling
- Like a spiritual prescription — guided and personal
- Risk: Scales poorly if pastors have many members

**Option C: Hybrid (Recommended)**
- General library open to all members (self-service)
- Pastors can additionally prescribe specific confession plans
- Wizard assignments (REQ-5) already support this pattern

**Discussion Questions:**
- Should certain confessions be restricted (e.g., Spiritual Warfare only for mature believers)?
- Should pastors see which confessions members are engaging with?
- How important is pastoral oversight vs. member autonomy?

#### 2.2 Daily Confession Schedules (15 min)

**Concept:** Members get a structured daily confession routine, not just a one-time read.

| Schedule Type | Description | Example |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| AM/PM Split | Morning confessions + Evening declarations | AM: Faith & Favor, PM: Gratitude & Rest |
| Weekly Rotation | Different category each day | Mon: Health, Tue: Financial, Wed: Family... |
| 21-Day Plan | Focused confession track for a specific need | "21 Days of Healing Confessions" |
| Ongoing | Confessions tied to prayer entries, always available | Personal prayer journal confessions |

**Discussion Questions:**
- Which schedule type resonates most with your congregation?
- Should schedules be pre-built or customizable?
- How does this connect to existing daily spiritual entry tracking?
- Should WhatsApp reports include the day's confession?

### Part 3: Features & Integration (20 min)

#### 3.1 Member Experience

**Scenario Walk-Through:** A member named Grace is going through financial difficulty.

1. Grace opens Prayer Engine → browses "Financial & Business" category
2. Sees 5 confession templates: Prosperity, Business, Job, Financial Breakthrough, Deuteronomy 28 Blessings
3. Selects "Financial Breakthrough" → sees confessions, declarations, scriptures
4. Adds it to her daily plan → gets AM/PM reminders
5. Her pastor also assigns her a "21-Day Financial Confession Plan"
6. Grace's daily entry now shows her confession engagement
7. Pastor can see Grace is actively confessing (without reading the content)

**Discussion Questions:**
- Does this flow feel right? What would you change?
- Should members be able to customize/edit confessions, or keep them as-is?
- Should there be audio recordings of confessions for members to listen to?

#### 3.2 Pastor/Bishop Experience
